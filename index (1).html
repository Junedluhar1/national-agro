Welcome to National Agro Industries

Leading manufacturer of quality agricultural instruments including Pavda, Khandi, and Khat. Located at Nandanagar near the railway station, we empower farmers with durable and reliable tools.

Our Products

Pavda

The Pavda is a traditional soil tilling instrument designed for efficient cultivation. Crafted with durable metal and a comfortable wooden handle, it helps prepare your fields with ease.

Khandi

The Khandi is a sharp and reliable harvesting tool designed to make crop cutting faster and safer. Its ergonomic wooden handle ensures a firm grip during long hours of work.

Khat (Charpayi)

Our Khat, also known as Charpayi, is a traditional woven bed crafted with sturdy wood and strong ropes. Perfect for farmers to rest comfortably after a hard day's work in the fields.
